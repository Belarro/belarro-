## SHOOTS

### 1. Pea Shoots (Erbsensprossen)

- **Category:** Shoot
- **Available sizes:** 225g (12 EUR), 450g (20 EUR)
- **Flavor:** Sweet, fresh, crunchy. Tastes like fresh spring peas.
- **Texture:** Tender stems with delicate leaves. Crisp, juicy bite.
- **Color:** Bright green stems and leaves, with curling tendrils.
- **Grow time:** 8-12 days from seed to harvest.
- **Shelf life:** 7-10 days refrigerated.
- **Best for:** Garnish & Salad. Pairs with seafood, Asian dishes, risotto, salads.

**Nutrition (per 100g):**
| Nutrient | Amount | % Daily Value |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Calories | 42 kcal | -- |
| Protein | 3.3g | 7% |
| Fiber | 2.6g | 9% |
| Vitamin C | 40mg | 44% |
| Vitamin A | 1087 IU | 22% |
| Vitamin K | 25mcg | 21% |
| Folate (B9) | 65mcg | 16% |
| Iron | 2.1mg | 12% |
| Manganese | 0.4mg | 17% |
| Phosphorus | 53mg | 8% |

**Key nutrients:** High in Vitamin C, beta-carotene, folate. Complete protein with all essential amino acids.
**Health benefits:** Immune support, anti-inflammatory, rich in antioxidants, supports eye health (lutein).
**Chef note:** Most versatile shoot. Works raw in salads, as garnish on soups, or lightly wilted. Tendrils add visual drama to plating.

### 2. Pea Salad (Erbsen-Salat)

- **Category:** Shoot
- **Available sizes:** 225g (12 EUR), 450g (20 EUR)
- **Flavor:** Sweet, mild, delicate pea flavor. Slightly milder than standard pea shoots.
- **Texture:** Soft, tender leaves. More leaf-forward than standard pea shoots.
- **Color:** Light to medium green.
- **Grow time:** 8-12 days.
- **Shelf life:** 7-10 days refrigerated.
- **Best for:** Salad base or mix-in. Lighter and more delicate than standard pea shoots.

**Nutrition:** Similar profile to Pea Shoots (see above). Slightly lower stem-to-leaf ratio means marginally higher vitamin density per gram of leaf.

**Chef note:** Use as a salad green replacement. Milder flavor makes it more approachable for guests unfamiliar with microgreens. Excellent as a bed for tartare or carpaccio.

### 3. Sunflower (Sonnenblume)

- **Category:** Shoot
- **Available sizes:** 225g (12 EUR), 450g (20 EUR)
- **Flavor:** Nutty, slightly sweet, with a sunflower seed aftertaste. Light and crispy.
- **Texture:** Thick, crunchy stems with broad cotyledon leaves. Substantial mouthfeel.
- **Color:** Deep green leaves on pale green/white stems. Sometimes with black seed hull still attached.
- **Grow time:** 10-12 days from seed to harvest.
- **Shelf life:** 7-10 days refrigerated.
- **Best for:** Garnish & Salad. Adds substance and crunch. Popular in health bowls and sandwiches.

### 4. Popcorn / Corn Shoots (Mais)

- **Category:** Shoot
- **Available sizes:** 225g (12 EUR), 450g (20 EUR)
