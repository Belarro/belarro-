## THE REALITY CHECK

You visit a restaurant. You talk to a chef. You get a name + phone number.
Now what?

That chef is busy. They're in the middle of service prep. They'll forget you in 20 minutes. Their phone is full of supplier messages, staff group chats, and delivery notifications.

Your WhatsApp message is competing with ALL of that.

So the message needs to be:
- Instantly recognizable (who is this?)
- Instantly clear (what do they want?)
- Zero friction (what do I do next?)
- Looks good on a phone screen (no walls of text)

## THE CORE MESSAGE — What chefs actually care about

Chefs do NOT care about:
- Your farming process
- Your soil substrate
- Your brand story
- Your sustainability mission

Chefs DO care about:
- Will it make my plates look better?
- Is it fresh and consistent?
- Is it easy to order?
- Will it show up reliably?
- How long does it last?
- What does it cost?

**Your one-liner when texting a chef:**

EN: "Fresh microgreens, delivered every Tuesday. Free samples, no minimum, no delivery fee."

DE: "Frische Microgreens, jeden Dienstag geliefert. Kostenlose Proben, keine Mindestbestellung, keine Lieferkosten."

That's it. That's the pitch on WhatsApp. Everything else comes in conversation.

## PDF vs LINK — The Decision

**PDF is bad for phones:**
- Takes time to download
- Opens in a separate viewer
- Can't click anything
- Looks small on screen, need to pinch-zoom
- Chef has to save it somewhere to find it later

**Link is better:**
- Opens instantly in the phone browser
- Scrollable, tappable
- They can bookmark it
- Shows pictures of the actual product
- Always up-to-date (you change the site, they see the change)

**THE RULE:**
- First message: NEVER send a PDF. Send a link.
- Price list: Send link to varieties page + type out the price summary in the message itself (chefs want to see numbers NOW, not click around)
- PDF only when: chef specifically asks for a PDF to print or share with their purchasing person

**What we need to fix on the website for this to work:**
- The varieties page currently shows NO prices and NO sizes
- A chef clicking belarro.com/varieties sees beautiful photos but can't make a buying decision
- We need to either: (A) add prices to the varieties page, or (B) keep prices off the site but always include them in the WhatsApp message

**My recommendation:** Keep prices OFF the public website (you said this is intentional — competitors shouldn't see them). But ALWAYS type the relevant prices directly in your WhatsApp messages. The chef gets numbers without needing to open anything.

## THE COMPLETE POST-VISIT FLOW

### STEP 0: While at the restaurant (before you leave)

What you need to collect:
- Chef's first name
- WhatsApp number (ask: "Can I send you info on WhatsApp?")
- What cuisine / what style of plating
- Any specific interest they mentioned
- Language preference (did they speak German or English?)

What you should leave behind:
- NOTHING physical. No business card, no flyer, no PDF.
- Why? Physical things get lost in a kitchen in 5 minutes.
- Your WhatsApp message IS your business card. It has your name, your link, and they can reply to it anytime.

### STEP 1: First WhatsApp (within 1 hour of leaving)

**Timing matters.** Not "same day." Within ONE HOUR. While they still remember your face.

**The message (DE):**

---
Hi [Name], hier ist [Your name] von Belarro.

Hat mich gefreut dich bei [Restaurant] kennenzulernen.

Frische Microgreens, jeden Dienstag direkt in deine Kueche:
- Sprossen (Erbse, Sonnenblume) — 225g 12EUR / 450g 20EUR
- Mikrogreens (Brokkoli, Rettich, Rucola + 10 mehr) — 113g 12EUR / 225g 20EUR
- Feinkraeuter (Koriander, Dill, Kapuzinerkresse + 5 mehr) — 12EUR pro Schale
- Alles zzgl. MwSt. Keine Mindestbestellung. Keine Lieferkosten.

Gerne bringe ich dir Dienstag eine kostenlose Probebox vorbei. Welche Uhrzeit passt?

belarro.com/varieties
---

**The message (EN):**

---
Hi [Name], this is [Your name] from Belarro.

Great meeting you at [Restaurant] today.

Fresh microgreens, delivered every Tuesday straight to your kitchen:
- Shoots (Pea, Sunflower) — 225g 12EUR / 450g 20EUR
- Microgreens (Broccoli, Radish, Arugula + 10 more) — 113g 12EUR / 225g 20EUR

**Why this works:**
- Identifies you immediately (name + company + where you met)
- Prices are RIGHT THERE in the message — no clicking needed
- One clear question at the end (time, not "are you interested?")
- Link at the bottom for if they want to browse more
- Short enough to read in 15 seconds

1. Walk in. Ask for the kitchen or the chef. If someone at the bar/host stand stops you:

**DE:** "Hi, ich bin [name] von Belarro. Wir bauen Microgreens an hier in Berlin und beliefern Kuechen in der Gegend. Ist der Kuechenchef gerade da? Dauert nur 2 Minuten."

**EN:** "Hi, I'm [name] from Belarro. We grow microgreens here in Berlin and supply kitchens in the area. Is the head chef around? Just 2 minutes."

**DE:** "Wir liefern frische Microgreens jeden Dienstag direkt in die Kueche. Alles vom Feld, am Morgen geerntet. 24 Sorten, keine Mindestbestellung. Ich kann dir gerne eine kostenlose Probebox vorbeibringen damit du es ausprobieren kannst."

4. Leave. Don't overstay. The pitch is the sample, not the conversation.
