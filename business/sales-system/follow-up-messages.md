# BELARRO — WhatsApp Follow-Up Templates
# All messages. Both languages. Ready to copy-paste.
# Updated: March 2026

---

## RULES

1. Send links, NOT PDFs. Chefs are on their phone.
2. Always type prices in the message — don't make them click to find out.
3. Max 4 short paragraphs per message. This is WhatsApp, not email.
4. One question per message. Not two. One.
5. First message within 1 HOUR of meeting them. Not "same day."
6. After 3 unanswered messages: STOP. Re-engage in 2-3 weeks with something new.
