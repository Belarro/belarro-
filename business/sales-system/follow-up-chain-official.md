# BELARRO — Official Follow-Up Chain
> These are the approved messages. Use exactly as written.
> EN = "Clude short" | DE = "Claude DE"
> 5 steps. Each has a name, a purpose, and a timing.

---

## Rules
1. Send within the timing window. Not earlier, not much later.
2. Always use [Link] = belarro.com/varieties (or inventory page when new site launches)
3. After Step 5 with no reply → STOP. Move to dormant. Re-engage in 6-8 weeks with something new.
4. If they reply at any step → switch to conversation mode, stop the chain.
5. Sign as "Ron" or "Ron von Belarro" — not "the Belarro team."
