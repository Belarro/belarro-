## THE THREE RULES FOR APRIL

1. **Post on schedule. No excuses.** 8 Instagram posts, 5 LinkedIn posts, 3 Stories. If you miss one, catch up the next day. Never let 3 days pass without a post.

2. **Engage more than you post.** If you post 3 times but don't comment on anyone else's content, you're invisible. The comments and follows are what make people discover you.

3. **Send the DMs.** This is where clients actually come from in Month 1. The posts build the profile. The DMs bring the people.
