# Belarro Marketing Blitz — 6-Month Strategy

**Goal:** Make every chef and gastronomy professional in Berlin know Belarro exists.
**Timeline:** March–September 2026 (Phase 1: 3 months, Phase 2: 3 months)
**Target:** 20 active restaurant clients by September 2026 (currently: 3)
**Budget:** ~150–300 EUR/month (5–10 EUR/day ads) + 100 EUR per successful referral
**Content engine:** AI-generated copy + founder-shot photos/videos

---

## The Core Message (every piece of content orbits this)

**DE:** "Microgreens aus Berlin. Am Morgen geerntet, mittags in deiner Küche."
**EN:** "Berlin-grown microgreens. Harvested this morning, in your kitchen by noon."

**Why it works:** Freshness is unchallengeable. Import competitors can't say this. The local farm can't match 24+ varieties. Metro can't match same-day harvest.

## Phase 1: Weeks 1–12 (March–June 2026)

### The "Berlin's Farm" Campaign

**Theme:** Position Belarro as Berlin's own microgreens farm. Hyper-local. The farm the city's best kitchens trust.

### Platform Strategy

#### Instagram (PRIMARY — where chefs actually scroll)

**Posting:** 5x/week (AI-written captions, founder-shot content)
**Ad spend:** 5 EUR/day targeting Berlin gastronomy professionals
**Content mix (Handley 4-1-1 adapted to 5 posts/week):**

| Day | Type | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Educational | "This is Red Amaranth. Peppery, crunchy, turns any plate into art. Harvested 6 hours ago." |
| Tuesday | Delivery day BTS | "Dienstag ist Liefertag. 14 Trays packed at 6am. On plates by noon." (video/reel) |
| Wednesday | Chef feature / testimonial | "Chef [Name] at [Restaurant] uses our Pea Shoots on every tasting menu plate." |
| Thursday | Farm / growing process | "Day 1 vs Day 7. From seed to plate in one week." (timelapse or side-by-side) |
| Friday | Soft sell / CTA | "Nächste Woche auf deinem Teller? Schreib uns eine Nachricht." |

**Reels (2-3/week minimum — this is where reach explodes):**
- 15-sec harvest clips (knife cutting microgreens, satisfying ASMR)
- Packing and loading the delivery van
- Chef plating with Belarro greens (collab content)
- Timelapse: seed to harvest
- "24 varieties in 60 seconds" showcase
- Side-by-side: Belarro vs supermarket microgreens

**Instagram Stories daily:**
- Morning: "Growing today..." (farm shot)
- Midday: "Delivering today..." (van/boxes)
- Evening: "On plates tonight..." (repost chef stories)

**Ad targeting (5 EUR/day):**
- Location: Berlin
- Interests: Professional cooking, restaurant management, gastronomy, Michelin, fine dining, chef life
- Job titles: Chef, Küchenchef, Head Chef, Sous Chef, Restaurant Owner, F&B Manager
- Lookalike: Based on website visitors + Instagram engagers

#### TikTok (REACH ENGINE — viral potential, chefs are there)

**Posting:** 4-5x/week (short-form video, reuse Instagram Reels + TikTok-native content)
**Ad spend:** None initially (organic only, TikTok rewards new accounts)

**Content pillars:**
1. **Farm ASMR** — Harvesting sounds, soil closeups, water misting, cutting. These go viral.
2. **"Did you know?"** — "Most microgreens in Berlin restaurants flew in from the Netherlands 3 days ago. Ours were in the soil 6 hours ago."
3. **Chef collabs** — Film chefs plating with your greens. Tag them. They share it.
4. **Behind the scenes** — Raw, unpolished. "4:30am. Time to harvest." Authenticity wins on TikTok.
5. **Satisfying visuals** — Colorful microgreen trays, rainbow of varieties, close-up textures.
6. **Controversial takes** — "Your garnish is dead and everyone can see it." Bold hooks that start conversations.

**Hashtags:** #microgreens #berlinfood #berlingastro #farmtotable #cheflife #finedining #berlinkitchen #microgreen #freshfood #localfarm #belarro

**Posting:** 3x/week
**Ad spend:** 5 EUR/day (when budget allows, alternate with Instagram)

**Content types:**

**LinkedIn targeting (ads):**
- Location: Berlin
- Industry: Food & Beverages, Restaurants, Hospitality
- Job titles: Chef, Executive Chef, Restaurant Owner, F&B Director, Kitchen Manager, Gastronom, Restaurantbesitzer

### The Referral Program

**Offer:** 100 EUR cash to any chef who refers a restaurant that:
- Works with Belarro for minimum 1 month
- Orders minimum 100 EUR in that month

**How to promote:**
- Mention to every existing client personally (WhatsApp)
- Print simple referral cards to leave at restaurants
- Post about it on Instagram/LinkedIn once a month
- "Know a chef who cares about freshness? Introduce us. 100€ for you."

**Growth loop (Andrew Chen):** Chef orders → loves it → tells chef friend → friend orders → referring chef gets 100€ → tells more friends. This is a real growth loop.

## The Nuclear Options (High-Impact Moves)

1. **"Microgreens Challenge"** — Send a free box to 10 Berlin food influencers simultaneously. They all post the same week. Berlin food Instagram explodes with Belarro.

2. **"Chef Swap"** — Film two chefs from different restaurants both plating with the same Belarro variety. Side-by-side TikTok. Tags both restaurants. Both reshare.

3. **"Fresh Test"** — Film a side-by-side: Belarro microgreens vs supermarket/wholesale microgreens after 3 days, 5 days, 7 days. The visual speaks for itself.

4. **"Farm-to-Table in 6 Hours"** — Time-stamped video: 6am harvest, 7am pack, 9am deliver, 12pm on the plate. Entire journey in 60 seconds.

5. **"Berlin's Freshest Plate"** — Partner with one high-profile chef to create a signature dish using only Belarro microgreens. Document everything. Press release angle.

## Rules of Engagement

1. **Never post without a hook.** First line/first 3 seconds must stop the scroll.
2. **Every post serves one of two goals:** Brand awareness OR lead generation. Know which.
3. **Respond to every comment and DM within 2 hours.** Social media is social.
4. **Repost every single chef/restaurant that tags Belarro.** This is free content + relationship building.
5. **German for Berlin audience, English for international reach.** Instagram/TikTok: mostly German. LinkedIn: bilingual.
6. **No stock photos. Ever.** Only real farm, real greens, real kitchens.
7. **Consistency > perfection.** A slightly imperfect post every day beats a perfect post once a week.
